Therapists Available

Xena

She isn’t a warrior princess, but she is quite the lover. Say hello to Xena! This big girl is rather bouncy when she first meets you. She is just excited to make a new friend and maybe get a yummy treat from you. She is a pretty good walker, although she can pull some. This girl just has places to go and people to see after all. Xena does well on stairs and she loves to check out all the new smells we have here in the city. She also has her fun side where she runs around just acting silly. She is a beauty and she is hard to pass up. If you are interested in meeting Xena, please book a session.

Dandylion

Vocal and confident, Dandylion is a bright girl that is looking for love. A connoisseur of head butts, she wants to be where you are and will stick to you like glue. She is not afraid to ask for attention and is often found perched in a cat tree sleeping. Never fear, Dandylion is instantly awake and active once a human is around. Looking for a snuggly companion that wants to be in your lap all day? Dandylion is just a session-booking away.

Thumper

This great chinchilla loves being scratched behind his ears and napping on a lap. Is very sweet and even though he has only one front paw, it does not slow him down. He loves to eat, play, and take dust baths all day. If you’re looking for an extra cute dust-bunny, Thumper is available for therapy sessions now.

Cali

Cali is a friendly and affectionate gal, and while she is a mature lady of about 10 years, she plays like a kitten! As expected with a Manx, she has a cute bobbed tail. Cali is up for anywhere that she can have cuddles and lap time with humans. If you are up for cuddles and lap time, sign up for a session with Cali.
